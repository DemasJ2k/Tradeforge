Fix V3 backtest engine: eliminate look-ahead bias + realistic costs

Major backtest accuracy improvements:

1. DEFERRED MARKET ENTRIES (eliminates look-ahead bias):
   - Strategy evaluates at bar[i] close, but entry now fills at bar[i+1] open
   - Previously filled at bar[i] open (strategy could see close then time-travel
     back to fill at open — impossible in live trading)
   - Exit orders still fill immediately (no look-ahead concern for exits)
   - New _fill_deferred_entries() processes pending entries each bar

2. ENTRY COMMISSION NOW DEDUCTED:
   - Entry-side commission was tracked but never deducted from portfolio balance
   - Only exit commission was applied to PnL — underreporting costs by ~50%
   - Now deducts entry commission via portfolio.apply_pnl(-commission)

3. REALISTIC DEFAULT COSTS:
   - spread_points: 0.0 → 0.3 (≈$30/lot gold round-trip)
   - commission_per_lot: 0.0 → 7.0 ($7/lot round-trip)
   - slippage_pct: 0.0 → 0.02 (0.02% per fill)
   - Applied to both BacktestRequest and WalkForwardRequest schemas

4. ONNX MODEL FILES ADDED TO REPO:
   - .gitignore updated to allow *.onnx, *.onnx.data, *.npz files
   - rl_lw_us30, rl_lw_xauusd, rl_mb_btcusd models included (~300KB total)
   - Enables RL inference on Render without external file storage
