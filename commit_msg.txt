Phase 3: LSTM/GRU price range forecasting + dynamic SL/TP

Backend:
- New: LSTMForecaster class with LSTM/GRU architecture (hidden=128,
  layers=2, dropout=0.2). Predicts future price distribution (mean,
  std, p20, p80) for dynamic SL/TP placement
- Train with PyTorch locally, export ONNX for Render inference
- Agent engine: load LSTM forecaster, adjust SL/TP based on predicted
  price ranges (BUY: TP at p80, SL at p20; SELL: inverse)
- API: POST /api/ml/lstm/train, GET /api/ml/lstm/predict/{symbol}
- New: scripts/train_lstm_local.py for standalone local training

Frontend:
- ML Lab: add Forecast view with LSTM/GRU training form (cell type,
  hidden size, seq length, horizon, epochs)
- ML Lab: display training results (val loss, direction accuracy,
  loss curve bars)
- ML Lab: show forecast with price distribution, predicted range,
  and derived long/short SL/TP levels
